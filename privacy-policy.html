Privacy Policy

Introduction

At vClock, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online clock service.

Information We Collect

  • Browser-stored preferences (like your selected timezone)
  • Temporary alarm settings (stored locally in your browser)
  • Basic usage analytics to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide accurate time information
  • Improve our services based on usage patterns

Data Storage

All user preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser. We do not maintain any personal information on our servers.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only to maintain basic functionality and remember your preferences.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any information with third parties. Our service operates independently without external data sharing.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes through our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy policy, please contact us.
